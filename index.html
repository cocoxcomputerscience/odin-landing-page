Who is Taylor Switch?

Taylor Switch is a world-renowned songwriter and singer, who has multiple critically acclaimed albums. She is an inspiration to many young artists out there and has created a legacy through her talents. She is, without doubt, the artist of the century.

Albums

Clouds

Clouds conveys a meaningful purpose of what people consider mundane in life.

Desperation

Money, love, fame. Desperation details what Taylor Switch wants to reach as an artist.

Drowning

An album filled with the passions and hardship of a failing relationship.

Moon

Tranquility. Moon is all about peace, calm, and nothing more beyond that.

Songwriting has been my passion since I was 5 years old. I wish to spread the stories that I've experienced through my music. Whether it be a crutch to rely on, music to dance to, or something that brings joy to people--as long as my music has a postive effect, that's all that matters.

-Taylor Switch
